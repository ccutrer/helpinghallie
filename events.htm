    • Saturday, August 29, 2009 - 8:00am-4:00pm
    • Friday, September 4, 2009 - 10:00am-2:00pm

    Thank you all to your incredible support in helping us get the word out about our benefit yard sale that we did for Hallie. What started as a small thing to maybe earn enough money to take her back to Salt Lake for some tests turned into something more than we could ever have dreamed of! It was a long month of sorting, organizing, and pricing, as well as living with all of the donations in my house and garage, but well worth it in the end. We surpassed our goals time and time again. So thank you to everyone for your help and support!
